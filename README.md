
# Car Sales Performance Dashboard 

## 🏎️ Project Overview

This interactive Tableau dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of car sales performance over a two-year period (2020-2021). It is designed to track critical sales KPIs, understand consumer preferences in car features (body style, color, transmission), and monitor regional performance trends.

-----

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks **Year-To-Date (YTD)** metrics with integrated **Year-on-Year (YoY)** growth indicators:

  * **YTD Total Sales:** **$371.19M** (⬆ 23.59% YoY Growth)
  * **YTD Average Price:** **$27.99K** (⬇ 0.79% YoY Decline)
  * **YTD Cars Sold:** **13.26K units** (⬆ 24.57% YoY Growth)

-----

## 🔍 Visual Analysis & Insights

### 1\. Sales Trends

  * **Weekly Trend:** A dynamic area chart tracking sales fluctuations throughout the year, peaking at **$15.53M** in a single week.
  * **Company Performance:** A detailed grid showing sales by manufacturer. **Chevrolet**, **Dodge**, and **Ford** emerge as top contributors by volume and total sales value.

### 2\. Inventory & Design Preferences

  * **Body Style:** **SUVs** are the leading revenue generator ($99.89M), followed closely by **Hatchbacks** ($82.77M).
  * **Color Popularity:** **Pale White** is the most preferred car color, accounting for **$174.53M** in sales, nearly double that of the next highest color, Black.
  * **Transmission:** A regional breakdown shows a consistent preference for **Automatic** transmissions over Manual across all major cities (Austin, Janesville, etc.).

### 3\. Regional Breakdown

  * **Austin** leads the regions in total sales revenue ($65.04M combined), maintaining a strong lead in both transmission types.

-----

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation

  * **Dynamic Filters:** Users can slice data by Date, Transmission, Body Style, Engine Type, and Gender.
  * **Calculated Fields:** Used to determine YoY Growth percentages and YTD totals.
  * **Custom Tooltips:** Enhanced data points providing deeper context on hover.
  * **Formatting:** A "Dark Mode" aesthetic designed for high-impact executive presentations.

-----


## 💡 Key Takeaways

1.  **Growth vs. Price:** While the volume of cars sold increased significantly (\~24.6%), the average price per vehicle remained stagnant, suggesting growth is driven by volume rather than price hikes.
2.  **Color Matters:** Pale White vehicles significantly outperform other colors, indicating an inventory priority for dealerships.
3.  **Market Leader:** SUVs and Hatchbacks dominate the market share, making up nearly 50% of total YTD sales.
